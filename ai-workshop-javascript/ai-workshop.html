🎯 Part 1: Introduction to AI (10 minutes)

What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. In this workshop, we'll focus on Machine Learning - a subset of AI where computers learn patterns from data.

2 Types of (Supervised) Machine Learning:

  • Classification: Predicting categories (e.g., iris flower species)
  • Regression: Predicting numerical values (e.g., house prices)
